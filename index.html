About

Tim and Chrissy are husband and wife who have been working on music together since they were in high school. They met by doing the music for their church and have been providing the music for weddings and masses for over 12 years now.

Although raised in the Catholic Church Chrissy and Tim embrace all religions and celebrations and are honored to provide the music for any special event.
