About

Horton Manor is a husband and wife duo who provide an intimate sound of acoustic guitar and vocals in the greater San Diego area. They specialize in wedding ceremonies, cocktail parties, corporate and special events.

Chrissy and Tim have been singing and playing professionally since 2002. They met providing music for local churches within Southern California. As of 2014, they have teamed up and began playing for wedding ceremonies and other special events.

In addition to music Chrissy has over 3 years of experience working as an Executive Assistant with one of San Diego’s top Wedding Coordinators, Très Chic Affairs. She understands how your event is unique and how vital every detail is.

There is nothing more intimate and personal than the sound of live music. It sets the tone for the night and is engaging to everyone in attendance. Horton Manor will personalize the music for each client.
